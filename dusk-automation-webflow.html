FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does setup take?

Most clients are live within 24 hours. The process starts with a 20-minute onboarding call where we learn about your business. We handle all the technical setup — you just add one line of code to your website, or we can do that for you too.

Do I need any technical skills?

None at all. We build and manage everything on our end. To install the chat widget, you or your web designer simply paste a small snippet of code into your site — it takes about 2 minutes and works with every website platform.

Will the AI sound like my business?

Yes — that's the whole point. We train the AI specifically on your services, your tone of voice, your pricing, your FAQs and your booking process. Customers typically can't tell they're talking to an AI, and if they ask, the bot is honest about it.

What happens when a lead comes in?

You get an instant SMS notification to your phone with the lead's name, contact details, and what they enquired about. All conversations are also stored in your dashboard so you can review them any time and follow up if needed.

What if I want to change the bot's responses?

Growth and Pro plan clients get monthly AI retraining included — just send us any updates and we'll have them live within 24 hours. Starter clients can request updates for a small fee, or upgrade their plan at any time.

Does the missed call reply work on my current number?

Yes. The missed call auto-reply works with any Australian mobile number. We set it up so that when a call goes unanswered, the system fires off a personalised SMS to the caller within about 30 seconds — no extra hardware needed.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No lock-in contracts, ever. You pay month-to-month and can cancel at any time with 30 days notice. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in. Most clients stay because they see results within the first week.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with any Australian small business that gets enquiries via their website or phone. Our most common clients are tradies, health & beauty, real estate, automotive, hospitality, and professional services businesses.
